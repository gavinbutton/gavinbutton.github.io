Welcome to the Project Netcrest Automation App

This automation app will allow you to generate management audit reports about energy monitoring within the Netcrest Ltd group.

The audit report will contain energy usage of the office over a specified time period helping to make Netcrest Ltd a carbon neutral company. You will recieve an email containing the reports as attachments.

To be able to use this app you will need:
  • A google account (your account will be used to send gmails containing the reports to yourself automatically).
  • To be an employee of Netcrest ltd that has been granted use of this app. Ask your manager for approval.
  • The app will need to be installed on your local desktop computer. Ask your manager for installation media.
